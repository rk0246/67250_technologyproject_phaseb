Our Mission

At MonoMuse, our mission is to celebrate the legacy, influence, and evolution of Metallica, exploring how their music has shaped culture and redefined the boundaries of heavy metal. We are dedicated to showcasing immersive exhibitions that highlight the band’s journey, from their thrash metal roots to their global impact. Through interactive experiences, live-performance simulations, and multimedia storytelling, we aim to engage visitors with the energy, innovation, and artistry that define Metallica. MonoMuse is a space for fans and newcomers alike to connect with the band's music, history, and enduring influence on the world of rock and beyond.



Milestones

  • 2020: MonoMuse Grand Opening
  • 2021: Launch of the first Metallica-themed interactive exhibition
  • 2022: Expansion of immersive experiences, including live performance simulations
  • 2023: Introduction of global exhibits highlighting Metallica’s cultural impact
  • 2024: Renovation of digital and multimedia installations for a fully immersive museum experience
